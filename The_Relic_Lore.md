LORE INFO

the original gods do not interact with the world enymore, although in times of crisis they can contact their children, this is how ambrias memories where returned
in this world, the end is not the same as in normal minecraft, its is an endless void/purgatory
after ambria and sickle where thrown into the end, all the gods lost their memorys and became mortals
somehow ambria ended up returning to the overworld in a smaller body, she slowly grew up in verdantia, but it had been 1000 years since she was cast into the void
eventually ambria started regaining her memories and began to confront other nations about the existence of the fourth god, which was sickle, but palios had erased the record of him existing
palios already regained his memory before ambria returned
eventually though after gree had also started regaining some memories, sickles powers began seeping back into the overworld, plant life was dying, and rot was spreading, infecting people too, making them sick
if someone died, they where unnaturally brought back from the dead, and became sickles pawns to control remotely
eventually sickle regained enough power and followers to where he was able to be found, and a fight went down, but it led to the death of sickle, palios, and gree
ambria still interacts with the world somewhat, but has tried to distance herslef more from mortal affairs, she is more there to oversee and step in if anything gets out of hand.


ORIGIN OF THE RELICS

long ago, before the original gods had their children, they helped mortals not by descending and walking among them, but by creating powerfull objects that mortals could weild, which granted them abilities
eventually though, once the original gods had their children, who did descend and walk among the mortals, their was less need for the relics, as the 4 god children could do far more than any object could.
the relics where forgotten and abandoned
however now that ambria is the only of the 4 remaining, and she is stepping back, there is a need for the relics again
across the world there will be shrines and ruined monuments where relics can be found
there are a vast number of relics, but a mere mortal cannot weild the power of more then one, it wil hurt them if they try
